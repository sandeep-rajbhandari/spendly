Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information that you provide directly to us. This includes your email address and password when creating an account, as well as any expense data you choose to log — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions of your transactions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data solely to provide, maintain, and improve the Spendly service. Your expense information is used to generate summaries, charts, and insights about your spending patterns. We do not use your financial data for advertising purposes or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in encrypted databases. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. You retain ownership of all data you input into the service, and you may export or delete your data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, and customer support. These partners have access to your data only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated to maintain the confidentiality of your information. We do not share your financial data with external parties for marketing purposes.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. These cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of the service. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may limit your ability to use certain features of Spendly.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You may also request a copy of your data in a portable format. To exercise these rights, please contact us through the application settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the application. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to addressing your concerns.
